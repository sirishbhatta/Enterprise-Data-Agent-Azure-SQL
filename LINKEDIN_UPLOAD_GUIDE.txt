TROUBLESHOOTING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  ❌ "Images aren't uploading"
     → Try refreshing the page
     → Check file sizes (should be 100-300KB each)
     → Use Chrome/Edge instead of Safari

  ❌ "Slides are blurry"
     → Don't worry! They're 1080×1350px (LinkedIn standard)
     → Looks sharp on phones (which most people use)

  ❌ "Text is hard to read on mobile"
     → That's why slides are optimized for 1080px width
     → All text should be readable on iPhone/Android
